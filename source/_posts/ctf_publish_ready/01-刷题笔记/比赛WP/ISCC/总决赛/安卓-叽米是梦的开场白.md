### **Exp（如有，请粘贴完整代码，不允许截图！）**

```python
with open("enreal", "rb") as f:
    data = list(f.read())

for i in range(len(data)):
    data[i] = (data[i] << 2) | (data[i] >> 6)
    data[i] &= 0xff
    data[i] ^= 0x44  
    data[i] = (data[i] >> 3) | (data[i] << 5)
    data[i] &= 0xff

with open("decode_enreal", "wb") as f:
    f.write(bytes(data))
```
